Privacy & Compliance

Privacy Policy – My Sovrano App

Sovrano LLC protects the privacy and reliability of every business partner using the My Sovrano application across the HoReCa ecosystem.

Last updated: November 2025

1. Information We Collect

We gather only the data required to operate your Sovrano partner account and make sure orders flow smoothly from creation to delivery.

  • Account data Company name, contact person, phone number, email address, and your unique Partner ID.
  • Order data Products ordered, delivery addresses, payment terms, and invoice records for accurate fulfillment.
  • Device data Device type, operating system, and app usage logs to improve stability and performance.

We do not collect personal data unrelated to business use and we do not process payment card information within the My Sovrano app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Sovrano LLC uses your data exclusively for partner services and to maintain operational excellence throughout the HoReCa supply chain.

  • Partner account management Keep your Sovrano profile, delivery preferences, and invoicing records up to date.
  • Order processing Prepare, dispatch, and complete your product orders without interruptions.
  • Operational communications Send delivery updates, digital invoices, product launches, and service announcements.
  • Continuous improvement Analyze usage data to optimize app features and partner satisfaction.
  • Compliance Meet accounting, tax, and legal requirements in every jurisdiction where we operate.

3. Data Storage & Security

Safeguarding your partner information is core to our service promise.

All business data is securely stored on Sovrano LLC servers located in Armenia. We apply industry-standard encryption, segmented networks, and restricted-access protocols to protect against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Data Sharing

Sovrano LLC does not sell or rent your partner data. Sharing occurs only when necessary to deliver the Sovrano service.

  • Authorized providers Sovrano logistics and IT partners operating under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Regulatory compliance Legal authorities, but only when disclosures are required by applicable law.

5. Account Deletion and Deactivation

Users have the right to request the deactivation of their account at any time.

To initiate this process, users must contact the application administrator by sending a request via email to info@sovrano.am or by calling +374 60 49 99 94. Upon receiving the request, the administrator may verify the user's identity for security purposes and will proceed with the account deactivation in accordance with applicable laws and data protection regulations. Once the account is deactivated, personal data will be removed or anonymized unless retention is required by law.
